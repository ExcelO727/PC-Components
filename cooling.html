Cooling

Fans

There are many components that generate heat such as the CPU, GPU, Power Supply, and many others. These components are the reason why cooling is very important in a PC, and is crucial for the PC to have along life span and survive for a long time. While every component does generate heat, some generate more heat than others. These components need their own dedicated cooling systems so they do not over heat and burn out the computer. Some of these components that need their own cooling systems are the CPU, GPU, and Power Supply. These 3 are the components that generate the most heat on their own and need cooling systems seperate from the main case fans that are used for the entire PC.
